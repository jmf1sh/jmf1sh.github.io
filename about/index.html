About

My name is Jonathan. After completing a PhD in pure mathematics at the University of Toronto I took a research postdoc position in GRK 1670 at Universität Hambug. Then I decided to do something a little bit more applied, so I took a postdoc position in scientific computing at the Jülich Center for Neutron Science. Currently, I do machine learning-related things at Cape Analytics. My primary interests are applied mathematics and high-performance computing. Feel free to add me on LinkedIn.
